My background

Thank you for taking a moment to read a little bit about me. In August 2020, I retired from the Air Force after 25 years as a Security Forces member, recruiter, OSI agent, and pilot. I'm now a Smokejumper Pilot with the US Forest Service out of Missoula, MT, where I live with my wife and four amazing kids. I've always been interested in technology and have worked with computers since I was 9 years old. Although my Air Force career did not allow me to pursue my passion for technology, I maintained proficiency by building my own computers, getting a Bachelors degree in Computer and Informaion Sciences, and getting an MBA with a certificate in Business Data Mining, primarily using SAS.

What's next?

At this point in my life, I'm ready to pursue my passion. I really enjoy flying, but my passion has always been in technology. At the completion of this program, I hope to be able to move into the technology field, either on a contracting basis, or as a full-time job.

Why SDGKU?

For all these reasons (and a few more), I decided to take the SDGKU FSDI program. I don't know if I'm going to make a career change in the Fall, but I do feel confident that, should I feel the need to do so, between my college background and my coding training from this program, I'll be successful in doing so.
